title: Remote Control
description: Control your locks remotely
---

With the Wi-Fi Gateway, you can:
- Lock/unlock doors from anywhere
- Check lock status in real-time
- Receive notifications


### Step 1

Purchase our gateway device. In order to use the remote unlocking feature, you will need to first purchase our gateway device. This device connects your lock to the internet and allows you to remotely control it using our Welock App.

!['sadf'](img.png)

### Step 2

Bind the gateway with your lock and Welock App. Once you have purchased the gateway, you will need to bind it with your lock and our Welock App. This process is simple and can be completed within a few minutes.


### Step 3
Switch to Wi-Fi remote mode. After binding your gateway, you can now switch to Wi-Fi remote mode by clicking on the Wi-Fi Box button located in the bottom right corner of the Welock App interface. You will see a signal strength indicator above the button.
